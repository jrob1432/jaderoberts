How to Overcome Cultural Bias in Voice AI Design
As Conversation Design Apprentice at Freshriver AI, Jade was part of two project teams. She brought in the Generation Z perspective. From there the team created personas and scenarios for a voicebot that addresses mental health and provides supplemental healing to two user groups. Each team member wrote individual dialogue flows and came together to decide which parts were the best to be included in the prototype flow.

The other project was to better inform prospective students about a specific college. For this, Jade reached out to three different user groups for user testing. She also collaborated on creating the tone and language choices for the bot.
