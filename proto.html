MusicSense
MusicSense was an end-to-end project for the Emergent User Interfaces Course at Aalto University. The concept is a glove that helps users make music regardless of musical knowledge and comprises of a full scale of notes with each finger on the glove corresponding to a different note. The glove also has the ability to increase volume and change the key.

This comprised of different colored circles to display on a laptop screen corresponding to each note the user plays on the glove. She also collaborated on user testing, concept creation and design and video prototyping as well as the write-up of the project.
In the Voice and Auditory Interaction course at Aalto University, Jade worked on creating voice and auditory museum experiences for the target group of low-vision individuals and for children. The prototype was done in Voiceflow. Jade collaborated on all aspects of the project including concept creation and design, prototyping and usability testing.
